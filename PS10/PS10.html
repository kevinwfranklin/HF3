Kevin W Franklin about
1020 Annunciation

Gallery + Lofts
The Warehouse District, once a thriving industrial sector in New Orleans, is in the process of gentrification. Old warehouse buildings are being converted into urban lofts and art galleries. This project, set right at the heart of the district, interrogates the relationship between art and living.

Location
New Orleans, LA
Year
Spring 2008
Type
Academic (second year)
Program
12 artist lofts, art gallery, coffee shop, & gym

Intersection
The two main elements (galleries + lofts) are intersected at the primary orthogonal corner of the site. This intersection forms an entrance at the public scale to an interior courtyard that functions as a communial space for residents, patrons of the coffee shop, and visitors to the gallery.
