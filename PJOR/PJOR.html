In the Shadow of Sawnee Mountain
This dental office was built on a small, pad-ready site near Sawnee Mountain—a stone's throw from the North Georgia Mountains. As such the office was designed in a mountain style, with earth-colored wood, brick, and stone.

The layout is compact, but allows for a circulation loop from check-in, to the treatment rooms, and to check-out. Although the site is small and constrained by setbacks, the building was designed for future expansion. 3 additional treatment rooms can be added to the back by relocating the lab and using it as a corridor.
